## 🎨 TONE & VOICE GUIDELINES

**Do:**
- ✅ Use clear, direct language
- ✅ Focus on measurable progress
- ✅ Sound calm and confident
- ✅ Lead with clarity and actionability
- ✅ Use examples to show range

**Don't:**
- ❌ Overpromise ("world-class speaker overnight")
- ❌ Use excessive hype or superlatives
- ❌ Make it sound exclusive/gated
- ❌ Use corporate jargon
- ❌ Ignore broad audience appeal

## 📊 CONVERSION PSYCHOLOGY

**Primary objection handled:** "Do I need to sign up?"
**Answer:** No signup needed to try. Create account to save progress.

**Emotional drivers:**
1. Confidence (aspirational outcome)
2. Clarity (immediate benefit)
3. Control (track your own progress)

**Trust signals:**
- Testimonials from beta users
- Clear privacy messaging (audio deleted after processing)
- Transparency about what scores mean
- No hidden requirements
